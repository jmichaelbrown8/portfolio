How I got here:

When I was much younger, I was a dancer, which led to an interest in music and sound, then film and photography, and now most recently software development. I've participated in all aspects of the development lifecycle as a web developer, software engineer, and Scrum Master.
My experience as Scrum Master has framed my view of software development to always focus on delivering business value above all else. I value reviews, feedback, and iterating on a design to improve the product. Now, I hope to refocus my career on the challenging and personally rewarding aspects of being a software engineer.

What guides my work:
